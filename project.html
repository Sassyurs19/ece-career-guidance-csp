COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECT

ECE Career Explorer Project

This project was developed as part of the Community Service Project to create awareness among school students about Electronics & Communication Engineering, educational opportunities and future career pathways.

🎯 Objective

Create awareness about ECE education, technologies and career opportunities.
